“Bhoomika: Where Soil, Soul, and Sustainability Unite.”

Bhoomika Centre, founded in 2003, stands as a beacon of hope and transformation in the picturesque region of Poonjar, Kerala, India. Our unwavering commitment to sustainable development and rural empowerment has touched the lives of countless individuals, creating ripples of positive change that resonate far beyond our immediate surroundings.
Our Mission: At Bhoomika, we believe that every community possesses the innate potential to thrive. Our mission is to unlock this potential by fostering sustainable practices, economic growth, and social well-being. We envision vibrant villages where individuals not only survive but flourish, and where the delicate balance between humanity and nature is preserved.
Native Windows: Empowering Artisans: One of our proudest achievements lies in the establishment of a network of native women entrepreneurs. These remarkable women, often hidden gems within their communities, now shine brightly through our “native windows.” Here, they sell locally produced food and exquisite handicrafts. These windows are more than mere marketplaces; they are platforms for rural artisans to showcase their skills, connect with discerning buyers, and earn a dignified livelihood. Through these windows, we celebrate tradition, creativity, and resilience.
Women’s Empowerment: Bhoomika recognizes that empowered women are the backbone of any thriving community. We provide training and unwavering support to women’s groups. These resilient women, once confined to the margins, now stand tall as breadwinners, entrepreneurs, and community leaders. Vocational training programs equip them with income-generating skills, financial literacy, and the confidence to navigate life’s challenges. As they weave their dreams into reality, they inspire generations to come.
Social and Economic Equity: Gender disparities persist, but Bhoomika is committed to dismantling them. Our organization actively supports the social and economic empowerment of women. By addressing inequalities head-on, we contribute to a more equitable society. When women thrive, families thrive, and entire communities flourish.
Nurturing the Earth: Sustainable Agriculture: The lush green fields of Kerala hold stories of resilience and struggle. Bhoomika works closely with local farmers, promoting sustainable agricultural practices. We advocate for organic farming, crop diversification, and soil conservation techniques. Our goal is not just higher yields but also a harmonious relationship between humans and the land. As we sow seeds, we sow hope for future generations.
Tourism with Purpose: Tourism can be a double-edged sword. Bhoomika collaborates with local communities to develop sustainable tourism products and services. Our vision extends beyond mere revenue; it encompasses cultural preservation, environmental stewardship, and community well-being. We believe that tourism should benefit everyone—from the tea picker to the storyteller, from the homestay owner to the forest guide. Together, we create experiences that enrich both travelers and hosts.
Impact and Success Stories: Our efforts have borne fruit. Families once trapped in cycles of poverty now send their children to school with pride. The aroma of freshly brewed tea mingles with the laughter of women at our training centers. Fields once depleted by chemical fertilizers now yield bountiful harvests. And when tourists arrive, they don’t just witness beauty; they become part of a larger narrative—a story of resilience, hope, and shared humanity.
Looking Ahead: As we tread this path of purpose, Bhoomika remains steadfast. We aspire to expand our reach, engage more stakeholders, and create lasting positive change. Our journey is not without challenges, but each challenge fuels our determination. Join us—whether as a supporter, a volunteer, or a curious traveler—as we continue to shape a more sustainable and empowered future.
